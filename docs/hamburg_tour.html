HAMBURG TOUR

Your chauffeur will pick you up at the central train station. From there, he leads you to some of the most famous attractions of Hamburg, where all eyes will be drawn to you.

First of all our tour leads you through Hamburg district St. Georg and then straight over the bridge, which separates the Binnen- and Außenalster. After this, you will drive past the Planten un Bloomen straight to the St. Michaelis Church. The tour goes on through the famous Speicherstadt, right to the Elbphilharmonie. This will also be our photo stop, where you will be able to take some beautiful pictures.

Now our tour leads you through the Hafen City and consequently along the Elbe, where you can also get a look at the Port of Hamburg. As our tour goes on, you will drive through the Old Town and the Sternschanze before we get to the St. Nikolai Church.

Further, your chauffeur will drive you right under the Landungsbrücken into the most popular district of Hamburg – St. Pauli and of course through the famous Reeperbahn. You will be dropped at the Beatles-Place, from where you can pick one of the many clubs and bars you will find there to celebrate until tomorrow!
