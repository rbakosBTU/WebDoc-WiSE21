DRESDEN TOUR

Your chauffeur will pick you up at the central train station. From there, our tour will lead you to some of the most amazing attractions of Dresden, where all eyes can only look at you.

At the begin our tour will lead you through the Old Town of Dresden right past the Stadion and the Big Garden on the other side. From there you will drive through the district of Striesen and straight along the Riverbank of the Elbe. As the tour goes on, you will drive past Brühl´s Terrace and the famous Frauenkirche. The next attraction will be the Semper Opera, which also is our photo stop, where you will be able to take some amazing pictures.

Now further, you will drive right to the Zwinger and through the district of Friedrichstadt. From there we will cross the Elbe to lead you through the Neustadt of Dresden. Here we can see the Golden Horseman, before we drive back into the Old Town.

There you will finally see the Royal Palace, which includes the Green Vault. From there, your chauffeur will lead you straight to the biggest club of Dresden – Kraftwerk Mitte. This club also has an indoor waterfall, which turns it into a perfect location to dance the whole night!
