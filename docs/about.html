Home About

Limotours is a car rental service, which was founded in Berlin in 2021. We are offering to rent the most luxury limousines including a chauffeur, who gives you an unforgettable tour through your city.

You can find us in Berlin, Hamburg and Dresden and each city has an unique tour ready for you. You just have to choose one of our amazing cars and the wild ride begins!

Our tours are perfect for various events of all kinds, for example memorable birthday partys, bachelor partys or even weddings. If you have some special wishes for your tour, feel free to contact us!
