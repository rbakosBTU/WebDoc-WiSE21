berlin_tour.html Tour in Berlin Contains detailed information about the tour in Berlin Overview of the tour in Berlin Your chauffeur will pick you up at the central train station. From there, you will drive to some of the beautiful attractions Berlin has to offer and where all eyes will be directed towards you.

At first, our tour will lead to Checkpoint Charlie. From there we will drive past the Victory Column and trough the most luxury street of Berlin – the Kurfürstendamm. Now our tour will lead you through Tempelhof and Kreuzberg, right over the Potsdamer Platz to the Brandenburg gate. This will also be our photo stop, where you will be able to take some amazing pictures.

Now our tour goes on, past the Reichstag and through the Berlin district of Gesundbrunnen, where you can also see the Memorial of the Berlin Wall. Further, you will drive through Prenzlauer Berg and finally past the Alexanderplatz to the Berlin Television Tower. From there you will also see the Red Town Hall on the other site. Our tour continues to the Berliner Dom and also past the Humboldt-University.

From there we will drive you through the Museum Island and again over the Spree to one of the biggest clubs of Berlin – the Soda Club. This will be our end station, where your chauffeur will drop you. This club has 5 different floors and is a perfect way to turn the night into day! 		picture_berlin.jpeg
hamburg_tour.html Tour in Hamburg Contains detailed information about the tour in Hamburg Overview of the tour in Hamburg Your chauffeur will pick you up at the central train station. From there, he leads you to some of the most famous attractions of Hamburg, where all eyes will be drawn to you.

First of all our tour leads you through Hamburg district St. Georg and then straight over the bridge, which separates the Binnen- and Außenalster. After this, you will drive past the Planten un Bloomen straight to the St. Michaelis Church. The tour goes on through the famous Speicherstadt, right to the Elbphilharmonie. This will also be our photo stop, where you will be able to take some beautiful pictures.

Now our tour leads you through the Hafen City and consequently along the Elbe, where you can also get a look at the Port of Hamburg. As our tour goes on, you will drive through the Old Town and the Sternschanze before we get to the St. Nikolai Church.

Further, your chauffeur will drive you right under the Landungsbrücken into the most popular district of Hamburg – St. Pauli and of course through the famous Reeperbahn. You will be dropped at the Beatles-Place, from where you can pick one of the many clubs and bars you will find there to celebrate until tomorrow! 		picture_hamburg.jpg
dresden_tour.html Tour in Dresden Contains detailed information about the tour in Dresden Overview of the tour in Dresden Your chauffeur will pick you up at the central train station. From there, our tour will lead you to some of the most amazing attractions of Dresden, where all eyes can only look at you.

At the begin our tour will lead you through the Old Town of Dresden right past the Stadion and the Big Garden on the other side. From there you will drive through the district of Striesen and straight along the Riverbank of the Elbe. As the tour goes on, you will drive past Brühl´s Terrace and the famous Frauenkirche. The next attraction will be the Semper Opera, which also is our photo stop, where you will be able to take some amazing pictures.

Now further, you will drive right to the Zwinger and through the district of Friedrichstadt. From there we will cross the Elbe to lead you through the Neustadt of Dresden. Here we can see the Golden Horseman, before we drive back into the Old Town.

There you will finally see the Royal Palace, which includes the Green Vault. From there, your chauffeur will lead you straight to the biggest club of Dresden – Kraftwerk Mitte. This club also has an indoor waterfall, which turns it into a perfect location to dance the whole night! 		picture_dresden.jpeg
